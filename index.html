Personal Cosmos

Personal Cosmos is a system which projects data from the earth and from other planets onto a sphere. We made this system with easily acquired goods and by developing an image conversion program. Personal Cosmos provides beautiful and realistic visualizations of data. It is an aid to more serious consideration of environmental and social problems. It is also a good resource for children to learn more about the earth and space. Personal Cosmos allows us to love data from space in a new way.
